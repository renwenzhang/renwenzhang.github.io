About Me

I am a PhD student in the Media, Technology, and Society program at Northwestern University. I work with Madhu Reddy in the PITCH Lab.

My research focuses on the role of technologies in facilitating health behavior change. I am also interested in peer support among people with mental illness using social media.

I hold an M.Phil. in Communication from The Chinese University of Hong Kong and a B.A. in Journalism from Shanghai International Studies University.


You can find detailed information about me in my CV.
