About Me

I am now a first-year Ph.D. student in Media, Technology, and Society of School of Communication at Northwestern University.
My research interests are social network analysis and e-health.

Before that, I received my bachelor's degree from Shanghai International Studies University and M.Phil from The Chinese University of Hong Kong in 2014 and 2016 respectively.
